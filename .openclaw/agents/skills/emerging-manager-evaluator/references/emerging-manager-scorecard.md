# Emerging Manager Scorecard
# Reference for emerging-manager-evaluator skill
# Detailed scoring rubric with worked example

---

## Overview

The Emerging Manager Scorecard is a four-dimension scoring system that replaces the five-dimension GP Scorecard used for established managers. The core difference: for emerging managers, there is no audited fund-level track record to evaluate. Instead, team quality and operational readiness fill the weight previously assigned to returns, alpha, and consistency.

The scorecard produces a weighted score between 1.0 and 5.0.

---

## Dimension 1: Team Quality (40%)

This is the most heavily weighted dimension because, for a first-time or second-time fund, the team IS the track record.

### Component Sources

| Component | Max Points | Source |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Prior employer quality (Tier 1-4) | 10 | Pedigree scoring in Workflow 1 |
| Role and seniority | 10 | Title, P&L responsibility |
| Years of experience | 10 | Total relevant CRE experience |
| Deals personally led vs participated | 10 | Attribution breakdown |
| Strategy alignment | 10 | Proposed strategy vs prior experience |
| Departure circumstances | 10 | Reference calls, diligence findings |
| **Total** | **60** | |

### Score Conversion

| Score | Pedigree Points | Reference Confirmation | Notes |
|-------|----------------|----------------------|-------|
| 5/5 | 50-60 | Lead role confirmed on 3+ deals | Exceptional team for emerging GP |
| 4/5 | 40-49 | Lead role confirmed on 2+ deals | Strong team |
| 3/5 | 25-39 | Positive but mixed references | Adequate; monitor |
| 2/5 | 25-39 | References raise concerns | Marginal |
| 1/5 | < 25 OR adverse findings | N/A | Do not invest |

### Automatic Score Reductions

- Any adverse background check finding (litigation, enforcement, fraud): score = 1/5
- Prior employer explicitly declines reference OR provides negative reference: maximum score = 2/5
- Team members have never worked together (no prior co-investment, employment overlap): maximum score = 3/5

---

## Dimension 2: Strategy Credibility (25%)

### Strategy-Experience Alignment (4-point test)

For each of the four core elements of the proposed strategy, score 1 for Yes, 0 for No:

| Element | Yes | No |
|---------|-----|-----|
| Principal has personally led a deal in this strategy type | 1 | 0 |
| Prior deals were in the proposed primary market | 1 | 0 |
| Prior deals were at the proposed fund deal size | 1 | 0 |
| Prior deals were in comparable market cycle conditions | 1 | 0 |

### Score Conversion

| Score | Alignment Test | Differentiation | Notes |
|-------|---------------|----------------|-------|
| 5/5 | 4/4 | Compelling; evidence provided | Best case emerging manager |
| 4/5 | 3/4 | Differentiation with some evidence | Strong strategy credibility |
| 3/5 | 3/4 | Weak or no differentiation | Capable but generic |
| 2/5 | 2/4 | Any | Material gaps in strategy alignment |
| 1/5 | 0/4 or 1/4 | Any | Strategy is aspirational |

**Automatic 2/5 cap:** GP has never personally led a deal in the primary proposed strategy.

---

## Dimension 3: Operational Readiness (20%)

### Operational Score (0-100) to Scorecard Dimension Score

| Operational Score | Dimension Score | Notes |
|------------------|----------------|-------|
| 85-100 | 5/5 | Institutional quality for emerging manager |
| 70-84 | 4/5 | Minor gaps; acceptable |
| 55-69 | 3/5 (conditional) | Requires completion milestones before final close |
| 40-54 | 2/5 | Material gaps; not ready for institutional capital |
| < 40 | 1/5 | Fundamental infrastructure missing |

### Conditional Pass

If operational score is 55-69 AND GP provides written infrastructure plan with specific milestone dates, score = 3/5 with condition note. LP should require confirmation of Tier 1 items before final close.

---

## Dimension 4: Fund Terms (15%)

### Market Benchmarks (Preqin Emerging Manager Survey, ILPA Principles 3.0)

| Term | Below Market | Market | Above Market |
|------|-------------|--------|-------------|
| Management fee | > 1.75% committed | 1.25-1.50% committed (step to invested post-IP) | < 1.25% committed |
| Carry | > 20% or below 8% preferred | 20% over 8%, European waterfall | 17.5-18% carry |
| GP commitment | < 1% or non-cash | 1-2% cash | 2%+ cash |
| Key person | Weak/soft suspension | Named persons, automatic suspension, LP vote | Additional protections |
| LPAC | None | LPAC with conflict authority | Broad LPAC authority, enhanced transparency |

### Score Conversion

| Score | Terms Profile |
|-------|-------------|
| 5/5 | All at market or better; GP commitment 2%+ cash; full LPAC |
| 4/5 | Most at market; GP commitment 1-2% cash; standard key person |
| 3/5 | One or two above-market terms; GP commitment 1%+ cash |
| 2/5 | Multiple above-market terms OR GP commitment < 1% OR weak key person |
| 1/5 | Excessive fees, non-cash GP commitment, no governance protection |

---

## Weighted Score Calculation

```
Weighted Score = (Team * 0.40) + (Strategy * 0.25) + (Operational * 0.20) + (Terms * 0.15)
```

### Verdict Mapping

| Weighted Score | Verdict | Action |
|---------------|---------|--------|
| 4.0-5.0 | EMERGING MANAGER INVEST | Commit; size within emerging manager tranche |
| 3.0-3.9 | CONDITIONAL | Commit subject to listed conditions; monitor closely post-close |
| 2.0-2.9 | WATCH | Decline; revisit at Fund II with demonstrated track record |
| 1.0-1.9 | PASS | Decline; team lacks foundation for institutional-grade fund management |

## Worked Example: Meridian Capital Partners Fund I

**Manager profile:** Two-person team (former Ares Real Estate VP and former Greystar portfolio director) spinning out to launch a $275M value-add multifamily fund focused on Southeast secondary markets (Huntsville, Chattanooga, Savannah, Charleston).

**Fund type:** First fund / spin-out (Ares friendly departure, confirmed)
**Vintage:** 2025 projected close
**Strategy:** Value-add multifamily, 100-300 unit assets, $15-$40M deal size, Southeast secondary markets
**Target fund size:** $275M

---

### Workflow 1: Team Pedigree Assessment

**Principal A (Managing Partner, former Ares VP):**

| Component | Score | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Prior employer quality | 10 | Tier 1: Ares Real Estate |
| Role/seniority | 7 | VP with deal leadership; not MD |
| Years of experience | 7 | 11 years |
| Deals as lead vs participated | 7 | 60% of attributed deals as lead |
| Strategy alignment | 10 | Value-add multifamily is exact match |
| Departure circumstances | 10 | Organized spin-out; Ares aware and supportive |
| **Subtotal** | **51** | Strong pedigree |

**Principal B (Operating Partner, former Greystar):**

| Component | Score | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Prior employer quality | 7 | Tier 2: Greystar (property management, not fund management) |
| Role/seniority | 7 | Portfolio Director, regional P&L |
| Years of experience | 10 | 16 years |
| Deals as lead vs participated | 4 | Operations role, not deal lead |
| Strategy alignment | 7 | Multifamily operations expertise; no fund management |
| Departure circumstances | 8 | Recruited by Principal A; amicable |
| **Subtotal** | **43** | Solid operating complement |

**Composite pedigree:** Average of 51 and 43 = 47, weighted 60/40 (Principal A as deal lead) = 49.

**Reference calls:** 4 calls completed: 2 Ares supervisors (both confirmed lead roles on 3+ deals); 1 co-investor (positive); 1 lender (positive, would lend again).

**Team Quality Score:** Pedigree 49 + lead confirmed on 4+ deals = **4/5**

---

### Workflow 2: Track Record Reconstruction

**Raw deal list from GP:** 12 deals (Principal A only; Principal B has no investment track record)

**After confirmation process:**
- 11 of 12 deals confirmed (1 excluded: prior employer could not confirm Principal A's role)
- Attribution discounts applied: 8 deals as lead (100%), 2 as co-lead equal (60%), 1 as deal team lead (50%)

**Adjusted track record (11 confirmed deals):**

| Metric | Unadjusted | Attribution-Adjusted |
|--------|-----------|---------------------|
| Equity deployed | $220M | $158M adjusted |
| Weighted avg MOIC | 1.74x | 1.58x |
| Weighted avg gross IRR | 17.2% | 14.9% |
| vs NCREIF NPI VA (SE) | +410 bps | +170 bps alpha |
| Hit rate (deals > 1.0x) | 91% (10/11) | 91% (10/11) |

**Survivorship bias check:** GP confirmed no omitted deals. 1 deal at 0.85x MOIC included.

**Track record assessment:** Adjusted alpha of 170 bps is solid. One confirmed loss is modest and disclosed. Survivorship bias check passed.

---

### Workflow 3: Operational Readiness

**Tier 1 assessment:**

| Item | Status | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Fund administrator | Contracted: Alter Domus | Tier 1 |
| Legal counsel | Kirkland & Ellis (fund formation) | Tier 1 |
| Auditor | BDO, CRE fund practice | Tier 1 |
| Key person provisions | Named (both principals); automatic suspension | Standard |
| GP commitment | 1.5% cash ($4.1M at $275M) | At market |

**Tier 2 assessment ($275M fund):**

| Item | Status | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Compliance officer | Outsourced (ACA Group); registering as RIA | Acceptable |
| CFO / outsourced CFO | Standish Management (outsourced CFO) | Acceptable |
| Cybersecurity | Written policy in place; MFA deployed | Compliant |
| BCP | Draft; to be finalized before first close | Minor gap |

**Tier 3 assessment:**

| Item | Status | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| LPAC | 3-member LPAC included in LPA draft | Best practice |
| Independent valuation | Annual third-party appraisal for all assets | Best practice |
| D&O / E&O | Bound as of first close | Compliant |
| Reporting | Quarterly reports; 45-day capital statements | Standard |

**Operational score:** 91/100. BCP gap (-9 points); all Tier 1 and Tier 2 items in place or credibly committed.

**Operational Readiness Score:** 91 = **5/5**

---

### Workflow 4: Strategy Credibility

**Alignment test:**

| Element | Result |
|---------|--------|
| Led deals in value-add multifamily | Yes (8 confirmed) |
| Led deals in Southeast markets | Yes (7 of 11 deals in SE markets) |
| Led deals at $15-$40M deal size | Yes (median deal size $22M) |
| Led deals in current acquisition environment | Partial (most recent deal 2023; current market is similar) |

**Score: 3.5/4** (adjusted to 4/4 for scoring purposes given 7-year track record in target strategy/market)

**Differentiation test:** GP asserts sourcing advantage in Huntsville and Savannah based on relationships with 3 local brokers (confirmed via broker reference calls) and prior acquisition history in both markets. Credible with evidence.

**Strategy Credibility Score:** 4/4 alignment + credible differentiation with evidence = **5/5**

---

### Workflow 5: Fund Terms

| Term | GP Proposed | Benchmark | Assessment |
|------|------------|-----------|------------|
| Management fee | 1.50% committed (IP), 1.25% invested (harvest) | Market | At market |
| Carry | 20% over 8%, European waterfall | Market | At market |
| GP commitment | 1.5% in cash | Market (2% is best practice) | Acceptable |
| Key person | Both principals named; automatic suspension; LP vote | Standard | Standard |
| LPAC | 3-member; conflict approval authority | Best practice | Best practice |

**Fund Terms Score:** Market fees, cash GP commitment at 1.5%, full LPAC = **4/5** (one step below 5/5 due to GP commitment below 2%)

---

### Scorecard Summary

| Dimension | Weight | Score | Weighted |
|-----------|--------|-------|----------|
| Team Quality | 40% | 4/5 | 1.60 |
| Strategy Credibility | 25% | 5/5 | 1.25 |
| Operational Readiness | 20% | 5/5 | 1.00 |
| Fund Terms | 15% | 4/5 | 0.60 |
| **Total** | **100%** | | **4.45** |

**Verdict: EMERGING MANAGER INVEST (4.45)**

**Conditions:**
1. Business Continuity Plan must be finalized and delivered to LP before first close.
2. RIA registration confirmed before first capital call.
3. Principal A to confirm in writing: no active non-compete from Ares (confirmed verbally; written confirmation required).

**Recommended allocation:** Within emerging manager tranche. Size at 50-75% of target emerging manager commitment given first-fund risk premium.

## Notes on Score Adjustments for Fund II

When a GP is raising Fund II with Fund I >75% realized, supplement the four-dimension scorecard with a Fund I performance assessment:

```
Supplemental Fund I Performance Score (same methodology as gp-performance-evaluator Workflow 6):
  Net IRR vs vintage: percentile ranking
  DPI vs vintage: percentile ranking
  TVPI vs vintage: percentile ranking
  Score: 1-5 using gp-performance-evaluator Returns dimension criteria

Revised Weighting for Fund II:
  Team Quality:           35% (reduced by 5%)
  Strategy Credibility:   20% (reduced by 5%)
  Operational Readiness:  15% (reduced by 5%)
  Fund Terms:             5%  (reduced by 10%)
  Fund I Performance:     25% (new dimension)
  Total:                  100%
```

This weighting transition reflects that Fund II GPs have demonstrated they can execute at least one fund cycle, shifting the balance back toward quantitative performance evidence.
