# Distribution Notice Templates

Reference for the distribution-notice-generator skill. Complete notice templates for each distribution type with worked examples, waterfall language, and capital account formats. Use these as the base templates; personalize per LP using the batch data from Workflow 4.

## Template 4: Return of Capital Distribution Notice

For distributions of invested capital without a disposition or income event. Explicitly distinguishes this from a sale or profit distribution to prevent LP confusion.

```
[FUND LETTERHEAD]
[DATE]

[LP LEGAL NAME AND ADDRESS]

Re:    [Fund Legal Name]
       Return of Capital Distribution Notice
       Distribution Date: [DATE]

Dear [LP Contact Name],

We are writing to inform you of a return of capital distribution
from [Fund Legal Name] (the "Fund").

--------------------------------------------------------------------
IMPORTANT: THIS IS A RETURN OF CAPITAL, NOT A PROFIT DISTRIBUTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------

  This distribution represents a return of a portion of your
  original capital investment in the Fund. It is NOT a distribution
  of profit or income. This distribution:

    - Does NOT represent appreciation or gain on your investment
    - Reduces your remaining invested capital in the Fund
    - Is generally non-taxable to the extent it does not exceed
      your adjusted tax basis (confirm with your tax advisor)
    - Does NOT satisfy or reduce any accrued preferred return owed to you

--------------------------------------------------------------------
DISTRIBUTION SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------------------------------

  Distribution Type:              Return of Capital
  Distribution Date:              [DATE]
  Source of Funds:                [Fund reserves / Partial asset disposition /
                                   Capital account restructuring]
  Your Distribution:              $[XX,XXX.XX]

  Wire will be sent to:
    Bank: [BANK] | ABA: [ABA] | Acct: [...XXXX]
    Reference: [Fund] ROC Dist [DATE]

--------------------------------------------------------------------
YOUR INVESTMENT SUMMARY (UPDATED)
--------------------------------------------------------------------

  Original Capital Contributed:          $[AMOUNT]
  Prior Return of Capital Distributions: $[AMOUNT]
  Remaining Invested Capital (prior):    $[AMOUNT]
  This Return of Capital:               ($[AMOUNT])
  Remaining Invested Capital (after):    $[AMOUNT]

  Note: Your preferred return continues to accrue at [X]% per
  annum on your remaining invested capital of $[REMAINING AMOUNT].

--------------------------------------------------------------------
PREFERRED RETURN STATUS (UNCHANGED)
--------------------------------------------------------------------

  Annual Preferred Return Rate:   [X.X]%
  Pref accrual basis after this distribution: $[REMAINING CAPITAL]
  Accrued unpaid preferred return:            $[AMOUNT]
  (This distribution does not affect preferred return accrual or
   payoff sequence per Section [X] of the LPA.)

--------------------------------------------------------------------
WATERFALL NOTE
--------------------------------------------------------------------

  Return of capital distributions are made outside the normal
  profit-sharing waterfall. No GP promote is earned or paid in
  connection with return of capital distributions.

  The waterfall for preferred return and profit distributions
  remains in effect and will apply to future operating distributions
  and sale proceeds.

--------------------------------------------------------------------
TAX INFORMATION (PRELIMINARY -- NOT TAX ADVICE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------

  Return of capital distributions are generally NOT taxable income
  to the extent they do not exceed your adjusted tax basis.
  Each dollar of return of capital received reduces your adjusted
  tax basis by one dollar. When your basis reaches zero, any
  further return of capital distributions will be taxable as gain.

  Your estimated adjusted tax basis before this distribution: $[BASIS]
  This distribution:                                          $[AMOUNT]

  [IF AMOUNT <= BASIS:]
  This distribution appears to be fully non-taxable.
  Estimated basis after distribution: $[BASIS - AMOUNT]

  [IF AMOUNT > BASIS:]
  IMPORTANT: This distribution may partially exceed your tax basis.
  Estimated basis: $[BASIS]. Distribution: $[AMOUNT].
  Estimated taxable portion: $[AMOUNT - BASIS]. Consult your
  tax advisor before filing.

--------------------------------------------------------------------

[Contact and signature block]
```

## Worked Example: 3-LP Fund, Full Sale Distribution

Complete worked example matching the scenario in SKILL.md (Riverside Industrial Fund II LP).

**Transaction facts**:
- Fund: Riverside Industrial Fund II LP
- Property sold: 789 Warehouse Drive, Columbus, OH
- Net distribution pool: $3,250,000
- Waterfall: 2-tier (8% cumulative non-compounding pref, then 80/20 LP/GP)
- Outstanding pref (all investors): LP001 $95,588 + LP002 $15,931 + LP003 $50,980 + GP co-invest (4.5%) $28,235 = $190,734 total
- LP investor pool: LP001 15%, LP002 2.5%, LP003 8%, GP co-invest 4.5% = 30% total
- Distribution date: March 25, 2026

**Waterfall calculation**

```
Step 1: Pref payment (all investors proportionate to ownership within 30% pool)
  Total pref outstanding (all investors, 30% pool): $190,734
  Applied to pref: $190,734
  Remaining pool: $3,250,000 - $190,734 = $3,059,266

Step 2: Profit split 80/20
  LP + GP co-invest share (80%): $3,059,266 * 0.80 = $2,447,413
  GP promote (20%):               $3,059,266 * 0.20 = $611,853

Step 3: Allocate investor pool ($2,447,413) by ownership within 30%
  LP001 (15/30 = 50.00%):  $2,447,413 * 0.5000 = $1,223,707
  LP002 (2.5/30 = 8.333%): $2,447,413 * 0.0833 = $203,918
  LP003 (8/30 = 26.667%):  $2,447,413 * 0.2667 = $652,580
  GP co-invest (4.5/30 = 15.00%): $2,447,413 * 0.15 = $367,112

Step 4: Add pref component to each investor
  LP001 total: $95,588 + $1,223,707 = $1,319,295
  LP002 total: $15,931 + $203,918   = $219,849
  LP003 total: $50,980 + $652,580   = $703,560
  GP co-invest total: $28,235 + $367,112 = $395,347
  GP promote: $611,853

Step 5: Reconciliation
  $1,319,295 + $219,849 + $703,560 + $395,347 + $611,853 = $3,249,904
  Rounding difference: $96 <- adjust GP promote to $611,949 or distribute
  rounding to largest LP (LP001: add $96 -> $1,319,391)
  Final total: $3,250,000 [RECONCILED]
```

```

  After paying preferred return, $3,059,266 remained. Per
```
